## 📌 The Problem

University students like **Priya**, a second-year Computer Science major, juggle **15+ WhatsApp groups**, miss important announcements, spend hours trying to find compatible study partners, and feel isolated as commuters.

**Core pain points:**
- 🔀 **Information Fragmentation** — important updates lost across platforms
- 🤝 **Manual Group Formation** — hours wasted finding compatible peers
- 🏝️ **Social Isolation** — commuters struggle to build campus connections
- 📚 **Academic Stress** — studying alone on hard concepts

---

## 💡 The Solution

Campus Connect is a unified platform with an **AI matchmaking engine** at its core. Students complete a smart intake form once; within 24–48 hours they are placed into a balanced 3–4 person study group scored for compatibility on skills, availability, and learning style.

### Core MVP Features
1. **🧠 AI-Driven Study Group Matchmaker** — ML algorithm + compatibility scoring (0–100%)
2. **📰 Unified Campus Feed** — filtered dashboard for academic announcements
3. **💬 In-App Group Messaging** — dedicated channels with file sharing
4. **🔐 University SSO Auth** — one-click sign-on, no extra passwords
